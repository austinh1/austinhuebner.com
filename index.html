Match Point

Jolly Crouton

Match Point is a frenetic couch competition game for 2 - 4 contenders. Tether, kick, block, and counter the ball into the opposing team's goal as you hop around in the newfound gravity of this modern take on a familiar arena.


Match Point began life as Gravity Pong, a simple physics-based multiplayer side project by Austin Huebner. Meant as an exercise in exploring game feel and tinkering with refining simple mechanics into allowing pro-level skill caps, the game seemed to be a hit at events, with crowds forming around and returning to whatever table held it. Austin joined forces with Clayton at GDEX, an Ohio-based game developer event. There, they discussed collaborating on projects, and thus began the overhaul and rebirth of Gravity Pong into Match Point. They’ve been working together on packing in lots of fun content to sweeten the pot of the elegant and catchy mechanic at the game’s core: tethering and kicking a ball. They never would have made it this far without chicken tikka masala dev nights.
